About Me

Stories are my passion: I can help make your stories amazing.

I love watching and making movies. My favorite film is Scott Pilgrim vs the World. Also, I have an intense codependent relationship with coffee. Outside of film, I love rock climbing, Twenty One Pilots, Eastern European video games (for some reason), hiking in the mountains and generally having adventures and learning new skills.

I have a lot of experience and expertise in many aspects of filmmaking. This means that when I create storyboards, I can envision how the whole project comes together and how all the different elements will contribute to the final experience for the audience. This makes my storyboards more useful to you as a filmmaker, makes the story more effective, and makes working and communicating your ideas with me much easier.
