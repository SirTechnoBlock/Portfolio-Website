Other Creative Work

Every aspect of filmmaking interests me, but I have as pecial interest in making action, comedy, and fantasy films. In addition to my own films, I love collaborating with other people on their projects and developing niche skills in the process. For example, some of the other things I do are:

  • Acting: I have acted in half a dozen different films in roles ranging from extra to lead, as well as in theater. It's always a thrill to understand and perform a complex character!
  • Feedback Sessions: I give feedback and workshop people’s scripts to refine the story and enhance the writer’s vision.
  • Stuntwork: I have undergone basic stunt training and have done some stunts in my own films.
  • Propwork: I enjoy creating props and practical effects from scratch.
  • Concept Art: I particularly like doing art for fantasy and sci-fi worlds.
  • Additional Roles: I have general experience across most roles in a film set, but I have a special interest in DP and Gaffer.
